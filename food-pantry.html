🍽️ Community Choice Pantry

Shop with dignity in a grocery store–style setting. Income-eligible Baltimore County residents choose their own food, tailored to their family's needs.

What to Bring

🪪Photo ID for all adults in household
📍Proof of Baltimore County residency (utility bill, lease, etc.)
👶Proof of all household members (birth certificates, school records)
💰Proof of income for all household members
🛍️Your own reusable bags — CAN carts must stay on site

Rules & Notices

✅ One visit per household per week
✅ Food distributed based on household size (1–6+)
✅ Grocery carts must remain on CAN property
✅ Sign in before cutoff time to receive service
✅ Pet food & hygiene items available while supplies last
✅ Item selection varies based on donations & inventory
✅ Respectful behavior required at all times
✅ Staff available to assist with additional needs
